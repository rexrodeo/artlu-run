Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What We Collect

When you purchase a race plan, we collect your name, email address, training city/state, and race goal time. Payment is processed securely through Stripe — we never see or store your credit card number.

How We Use It

Your information is used to build your personalized race plan and to send you your access code and plan-ready notification. We don't sell or share your data with third parties.

Cookies

We use a session cookie to keep you logged into your dashboard. No tracking cookies or third-party analytics are used.

Data Retention

Your purchase records and race plans are retained so you can access them anytime. If you'd like your data deleted, email us at vonrexroad@gmail.com.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Email vonrexroad@gmail.com.
